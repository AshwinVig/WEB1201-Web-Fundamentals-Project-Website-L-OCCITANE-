About Us

Our Journey In Provence

It all started at the Provence marketplaces in 1976. A 23-year-old named Olivier Baussan started distilling rosemary essential oil to sell locally with nothing more than an alembic, a tiny truck, and excellent plant knowledge. In order to provide natural beauty to homes around the world, he expanded his production from oils to soaps and creams. He did this by drawing inspiration from the land he grew up on.

Commitments

Creating Values Touching Lives

Our effort to give back is made possible by the L'OCCITANE Foundation, which was established in 2006. Our two main programmes help people with vision impairment all across the world and empower women economically in Burkina Faso. Additionally, our staff members take part in Foundation projects, which range from natural heritage in Provence to post-tsunami restoration in Japan.

Our Spa

Experience For Your Senses

The Spa is a logical extension of the L'OCCITANE brand. We strive for authenticity and always favour hand-performed massages over technologies, fusing our recognisable elements from the Mediterranean with conventional massage methods from throughout the world. Currently, our global network includes over 90 establishments, ranging from iconic "Spa by L'OCCITANE" locations in luxurious 5* hotels with soul to "Petit Spas" attached to our stores. Each provides a unique brand experience for all-around wellness.
